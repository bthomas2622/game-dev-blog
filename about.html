About

Ben Thomas (@freebrunch)

Favorite Games: Halo 2, Diddy Kong Racing, Starcraft 2, Pokemon Snap, Guitar Hero, The Witness, Gone Home

I was born in Ridgewood, NJ and grew up in Atlanta, GA. I have fond memories going to the pool during the hot summer days then staying up all night playing N64 and later Halo 2. Escaped the suburbs to pursue mechanical engineering at Georgia Tech. Since graduating have struggled to find my way, hopped between various mega corporations and changed career to software engineering.

I now reside in the Bay Area and outside my day job I like dreaming up fun game projects to learn skills for and work on. I love hanging out with friends, hiking, non-fiction books, and NBA twitter. I am passionate about urban planning, social justice, and oatmeal.

Love, Ben
