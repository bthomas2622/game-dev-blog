Nectar of the Gods

Platforms: Windows, Mac
Prototype Released: 11/15/2019

Nectar of the Gods is a 2D real-time strategy game about bugs addicted to human beverages. Align with a bug family, strategically deploy your units, and make sure there's not a drop of sugar left!

Space Canoe

Platforms: Windows, Mac, Linux, Android
Released: 1/6/2017

Space Canoe is a 2D retro arcade styled game. The player paddles the canoe away from the perilous space debris. The goal is to survive as long as possible in order to obtain a high "debris dodged" score.

Colony Collapse Disorder (CCD)

Platforms: Browser
Released: 8/19/2016

Colony Collapse Disorder (CCD) is a procedurally generated adventure game where you play as an Oakland bee scouring a flower field. You must maintain honey reserves, navigate randomized encounters, and most importantly produce Queen Bees.
